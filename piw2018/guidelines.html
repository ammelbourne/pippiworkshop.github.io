Poster Abstract Submission Guidelines

We invite submissions of short abstracts (up to 400 words/one page). These should be submitted electronically via the CMT system (link above).

Abstracts submitted before the 16th March will be considered for oral presentation and award of travel stipends. Abstracts may be submitted after this date for consideration of presentation as posters at the workshop

Abstracts will be reviewed and assessed for quality of work and a decision made on whether to include the work as a poster during the first-day of the workshop. We expect to select up to three interesting high quality abstracts for oral presentation.
