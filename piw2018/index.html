UCL CMIC Workshop on Placenta Imaging - 12th/13th April 2018

This workshop hosted by the Centre for Medical Image Computing, will bring together the most exciting recent research into placenta imaging in the UK. We will boost the interaction of UK researchers by hosting a public enagmeent session and an evening social event. The second day will host a debate about the use of placental MRI.

A printable flyer is available here.

We are inviting submissions of short abstracts for a poster session at the workshop. These can be submitted electronically via the link above. A selection of submissions will be chosen for award of travel stipends and oral presentation.

During the event we will host a research image competition. Please submit your interesting images via email to the workshop organisers with a short text describing how the image was made. These will be printed and displayed at the workshop.
